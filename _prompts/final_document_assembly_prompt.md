# Final Document Assembly Prompt

You are assembling a final synthesis report after human legal judgment review.

Required input:

- human legal judgment memo
- claim ledger
- rule anchor map
- synthesis risk review
- evidence manifest

Do not proceed if no human legal judgment memo exists.

Use only human-confirmed conclusions.

You may:

- organize structure;
- improve readability;
- preserve traceability;
- prepare a final synthesis report draft.

You may not:

- add new conclusions;
- strengthen conclusion language;
- remove limitations;
- remove pending verification notes;
- let output-layer language change synthesis-layer judgment.

The final report must preserve:

- evidence base;
- case observations;
- tentative patterns;
- rule anchors;
- human-confirmed legal conclusions;
- limitations and pending verification;
- traceability appendix.
